# Message Broker Terminology 
	1. Publisher & Consumer
		1 to 1 Mapping
	
	2. Publisher & Subscriber
		1 to many Mapping

	3. Routing
		1 Producer sending to multiple consumer based on certaing property.

	4. RPC - Request/Reply Pattern
		Producer sends msg
		Consumer sends ack once successfully consumed the msg
